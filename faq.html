Why does Good Leaf exists?

Good Leaf aims to share knowledge of edible plants that you could find at the corner of your street or during a walk in the forest, in the moutain, and in about every place in the world. These are thousands of edible plants other than the vegetables and cereals everybody knows!

Why are there Sign Up and Log In forms?

In the future, Good Leaf will allow users to save their favorite edible plants in a private and personnal place, that's why!

Would it be possible to have something else than an edible plant choosen randomly?

Yes, it will be possible. Right now there is only a random edible plant discovery option, but in the future you will be able to select a location and/or a period of the year to find edible plants available right here, rigth now. You will also be able to store them in your personnal page and display them according to your preferences.

How can we have so many edible plants data?

That is thanks to the great Trefle API!
